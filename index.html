My Story

My journey into data began with a fascination for discovering the story hidden behind numbers. I started in financial and market research, analyzing companies and trends to uncover insights that drive smarter decisions.

Wanting to go deeper, I pursued a Master's in Data Analytics and Strategic Business Intelligence at Long Island University, where I learned to transform complex data into powerful insights using analytics and machine learning.
